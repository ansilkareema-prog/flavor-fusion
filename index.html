Our Story

Cafe Rikka was born from a simple belief: great food brings people together. We're not just another cafwe're a cultural crossroads where global comfort food meets the vibrant energy of Indian caf culture.

From aromatic Arabic grills to indulgent premium shakes, from comforting butter chicken to refreshing mocktails, we've curated a menu that celebrates diversity. Every dish tells a story, every sip creates a memory.

Our space is designed for connectionwhether you're catching up with friends, working on your next big idea, or simply enjoying a quiet moment with exceptional coffee. This is more than a caf. This is your place.
